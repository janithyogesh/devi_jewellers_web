A Legacy of Excellence

Since 1994, Devi Jewellers has been a cherished part of the Embilipitiya community, located right in the heart of the town. Our journey began with a passion for creating not just jewelry, but timeless treasures that celebrate life's most precious moments.

Our master craftsmen are dedicated to blending the rich heritage of traditional Sri Lankan jewelry with the elegance of modern design. We believe in honoring time-honored techniques while embracing innovation. By combining the time-honored skill of handcrafting with state-of-the-art technology, we create pieces of exceptional beauty and quality.

Every piece in our collection, from intricate wedding sets to contemporary everyday wear, is meticulously designed and crafted. We are committed to using only the highest quality materials, ensuring that each item we create is a true work of art that will last for generations.

At Devi Jewellers, we take pride in our craft and are passionate about helping you find or create the perfect piece to tell your unique story. We invite you to visit our store and discover the beauty and tradition that define us.
